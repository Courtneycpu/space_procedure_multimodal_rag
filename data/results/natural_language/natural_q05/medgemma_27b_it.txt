    *   Place the Blood Pressure Cuff on the patient's bare arm.
    *   Press the [On/Off] button.
    *   Record the pulse and blood pressure from the display.
4.  **Record data:** Record all the measured data (Pulse, Respiratory Rate, SpO2, Blood Pressure) for later reporting to the Surgeon.
