The main items and tools needed to treat difficulty breathing are:

- **Respiratory Support Pack**: Includes a Cue Card and a Low Flow Non-Rebreather Mask for oxygen administration.
- **Medical Diagnostic Pack (Blue)**: Contains a Blood Oximeter, Blood Pressure Cuff (appropriate size), Blood Pressure Monitor, and Stethoscope.
- **Topical & Injectable Medication Pack (Brown)**: Includes Albuterol (Proventil HFA).
- **AA Batteries**: Four are required.
