**Leg Insertion Site:**
- Place finger on bony protrusion identified by circle (Figure 2)
- Move finger 2 cm towards the inner leg
- Move finger 1 cm up towards knee, identified by "+" (Figure 2)

**Setup Steps:**
1. Unstow Emergency Medical Treatment Pack (Red) containing:
   - Scissors - Trauma
   - Intraosseous Device Start Kit (Intraosseous Injection Device, IV Cap, Medical Tape, Syringe 10 mL)
2. Tear off two 10 cm pieces of Medical Tape and set aside
3. Attach IV Cap to Syringe (Figure 1)
4. Expose leg to knee using Scissors - Trauma

**Activation/Insertion Steps:**
1. Remove Intraosseous Device from packaging and place blue barrel on insertion point (Figure 3)
   - **WARNING:** Needle could eject from end of blue barrel becoming a projectile

**Post-Insertion Steps:**
1. Place safety latch between skin and hub of Intraosseous needle (Figure 5)
2. Place Medical Tape over safety latch to secure (Figure 6)
3. Stabilize Intraosseous needle with hand; with other hand, remove inner needle
4. Remove sterile cover from IV Cap, then attach Syringe and IV Cap assembly to Intraosseous needle (Figure 6)
