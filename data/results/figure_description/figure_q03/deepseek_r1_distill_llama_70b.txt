Answer:
The leg insertion site for the intraosseous (IO) device is located by placing a finger on the bony protrusion identified by a circle, moving 2 cm towards the inner leg, and then 1 cm up towards the knee, marked by a plus (as shown in Figure 2).

The main setup and activation steps are as follows:

1. **Prepare the Intraosseous Device:**
   - Unstow the Emergency Medical Treatment Pack (Red) and the Intraosseous Device Start Kit.
   - Attach the IV Cap to the Syringe 10 mL (Figure 1).
   - Expose the leg to the knee using scissors.

2. **Post-Insertion Steps:**
   - Place the safety latch between the skin and the hub of the Intraosseous needle (Figure 5).
   - Secure the safety latch with Medical Tape (Figure 6).
   - Stabilize the Intraosseous needle and remove the inner needle.
   - Attach the Syringe and IV Cap assembly to the Intraosseous needle (Figure 6).

**Insertion Site:**
- Place finger on the bony protrusion identified by a circle.
- Move finger 2 cm towards the inner leg.
- Move finger 1 cm up towards the knee, identified by a "+".

**Setup Steps:**
1. Unstow the Emergency Medical Treatment Pack (Red) and Intraosseous Device Start Kit.
2. Attach the IV Cap to the Syringe 10 mL.
3. Expose the leg to the knee using Scissors - Trauma.
4. Tear off two 10 cm pieces of Medical Tape and set aside.

**Activation Steps:**
1. Remove the safety latch and stow it for later use.
2. Maintain positive pressure on the blue barrel, keeping it perpendicular to the skin at the insertion point.
3. Place the palm of your other hand over the gray end of the Intraosseous Device and apply gentle pressure to activate. A click will sound when activated.
4. Gently lift the Intraosseous Device, exposing the needle unit.

**Warning:** The needle could eject from the blue barrel and become a projectile.

   - Place finger on the bony protrusion (identified by a circle).  
   - Move finger 2 cm towards the inner leg.  
   - Move finger 1 cm up towards the knee (identified by a "+").  

2. **Setup and Activation Steps for the IO Device**:  
   - Remove the Intraosseous Device from packaging and place the blue barrel on the insertion point.  
   - Insert the needle at a 90-degree angle with gentle pressure until it seats.  
   - Activate the device according to the manufacturer's instructions.  

**Warning**: The needle could eject from the end of the blue barrel, becoming a projectile.  

If unsuccessful, attempt to re-administer medication using the other leg.

1. **Leg Insertion Site:**

   - Move finger 2 cm towards the inner leg.

2. **Setup Steps:**
   1. Unstow the Emergency Medical Treatment Pack (Red).

   3. Attach IV Cap to Syringe (refer to Figure 1).
   4. Expose leg to knee using Scissors - Trauma.

3. **Activation Steps:**
   1. Attach Syringe 10 mL to IV Cap on the Intraosseous Device.
   2. Pull back on the plunger of the Syringe 10 mL by at least 5 mL, then disconnect it from the blue IV Cap. (This may clear bone fragments.)
   3. Remove Intraosseous Device from packaging and place the blue barrel on the insertion point.

**Warning:** The needle could eject from the end of the blue barrel, becoming a projectile. Ensure the blue barrel is securely placed on the insertion point.
