Answer:
1.  **Identify Intraosseous Site:**
    *   Place finger on bony protrusion identified by circle (Figure 2).
    *   Move finger 2 cm towards the inner leg.
    *   Move finger 1 cm up towards knee, identified by "+". (Figure 2)

2.  **Prepare Intraosseous Device:**
    *   Unstow Emergency Medical Treatment Pack (Red).
    *   Tear off two 10 cm pieces of Medical Tape and set aside.
    *   Attach IV Cap to Syringe (Figure 1).
    *   Expose leg to knee [Scissors - Trauma].

3.  **Prepare for Second Intraosseous Device Insertion (if needed):**
    *   Retrieve Intraosseous Device Start Kit with Second Intraosseous Device [Emergency Medical Treatment Pack (Red)].

4.  **Post Insertion Steps:**
    *   Place safety latch between skin and hub of Intraosseous needle (Figure 5).
    *   Place Medical Tape over safety latch to secure (Figure 6).
    *   Stabilize Intraosseous needle with hand; with other hand, remove inner needle.
    *   Remove sterile cover from IV Cap, then attach Syringe and IV Cap assembly to Intraosseous needle (Figure 6).

5.  **If First Device Fails:**
    *   Attach Syringe 10 mL to IV Cap on failed Intraosseous Device(s).
    *   Pull back on plunger of Syringe 10 mL >= 5 mL, then disconnect Syringe 10 mL from blue IV Cap. Blood or fluid may or may not be seen. This action will potentially clear bone fragments from the needle.

    *   Gently lift Intraosseous Device, exposing the needle unit.

Answer:
1.  **Identify the Intraosseous Site:**
    *   Place finger on the bony protrusion identified by a circle (Figure 2).
    *   Move finger 2 cm towards the inner leg.
    *   Move finger 1 cm up towards the knee, identified by a "+". (Figure 2)

2.  **Prepare the Intraosseous Device:**
    *   Unstow the Emergency Medical Treatment Pack (Red).
    *   Tear off two 10 cm pieces of Medical Tape and set aside.
    *   Attach the IV Cap to the Syringe. (Figure 1)
    *   Expose the leg to the knee using Scissors - Trauma.

3.  **Insert the Device:**
    *   Remove the Intraosseous Device from its packaging.
    *   Place the blue barrel of the device on the identified insertion point. (Figure 3)
    *   Push the device firmly and rapidly into the bone.
    *   Remove the outer needle.

4.  **Post Insertion Steps:**
    *   Place the safety latch between the skin and the hub of the Intraosseous needle. (Figure 5)
    *   Place Medical Tape over the safety latch to secure it. (Figure 6)
    *   Stabilize the Intraosseous needle with one hand.
    *   With the other hand, remove the inner needle.
    *   Remove the sterile cover from the IV Cap.
    *   Attach the Syringe and IV Cap assembly to the Intraosseous needle. (Figure 6)

5.  **Clear the Device (if needed):**
    *   Attach a 10 mL Syringe to the IV Cap.
    *   Pull back on the plunger of the Syringe >= 5 mL, then disconnect the Syringe from the blue IV Cap. (Figure 1)

6.  **Prepare for Second Insertion (if needed):**
    *   Retrieve the Intraosseous Device Start Kit with the Second Intraosseous Device from the Emergency Medical Treatment Pack (Red).
    *   Repeat steps 2-5 on the other leg.

**Warnings:**
*   Needle could eject from the end of the blue barrel becoming a projectile.
*   Remove the Intraosseous Device from packaging and place the blue barrel on the insertion point. (Figure 3) -> 4
