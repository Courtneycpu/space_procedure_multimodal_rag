**Leg insertion site**  
1. Locate the bony protrusion on the leg (shown as a circle).  
2. Move your finger **2 cm toward the inner leg** from that point.  
3. Then move the finger **1 cm upward toward the knee** (the “+” marker).  

**Main setup steps for the intra‑osseous (IO) device**  

1. **Unstow the Emergency Medical Treatment Pack (Red)** and retrieve:  
   - Scissors – Trauma  
   - Intra‑osseous Device Start Kit (IO injection device, IV cap, medical tape, 10 mL syringe)  
2. **Prepare tape:** tear off two 10 cm pieces of medical tape and set aside.  
3. **Assemble the syringe:** attach the IV cap to the 10 mL syringe.  
4. **Expose the insertion leg** (cut clothing as needed with the trauma scissors).  

**Activation (post‑insertion) steps**  

1. Place the **safety latch** between the skin and the hub of the IO needle.  
2. Secure the latch with **medical tape**.  
3. **Stabilize the IO needle** with one hand; with the other hand, remove the inner needle.  
4. Remove the sterile cover from the IV cap, then **attach the syringe‑IV‑cap assembly** to the IO needle.  
5. Pull back the syringe plunger **≥ 5 mL** (to clear bone fragments) and then disconnect the syringe as needed for medication administration.
