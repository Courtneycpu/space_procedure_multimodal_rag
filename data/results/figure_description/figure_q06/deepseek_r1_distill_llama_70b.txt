Answer:
After a lost dental crown, follow these steps to replace and confirm seating using Dental Adhesive:

1. **Prepare the Loose Crown:**
   - Remove residual cement using a Dental Carver File (G).
   - Check the fit by placing the crown on the tooth and having the subject bite down.
   - Dry the crown with Gauze 4"x4" and temporarily stow it in the gauze.

2. **Replace the Crown:**
   - Coat the surfaces of the crown contacting the tooth with Dental Adhesive.
   - Place the crown over the tooth, ensuring proper orientation.
   - Use a Cotton Swab: have the subject bite down on it and the crown for 3 to 5 seconds to seat it fully.
   - Remove the Cotton Swab and have the subject bite again to check if the crown is fully seated.

3. **Confirm Crown Placement:**
   - If fully seated, place the Cotton Swab back and have the subject bite down with moderate pressure for 2 to 3 minutes.
   - If not seated, carefully remove the crown using a Dental Crown Remover (D) and repeat the process.

This structured approach ensures proper replacement and confirmation of the crown's seating.
