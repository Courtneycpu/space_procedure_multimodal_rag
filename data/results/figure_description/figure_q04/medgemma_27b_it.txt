    *   Photo document the tooth and the repaired filling or crown or broken fragment or exposed pulp (depending on the specific dental procedure). Use the [Digital Camera].
    *   For Exposed Dental Pulp: Photo document the tooth and broken fragment [Digital Camera].
    *   For Temporary Dental Filling: Photo document the tooth and repaired filling [Digital Camera].
    *   For Dental Crown Replacement: Photo document the tooth and repaired crown [Digital Camera].
    *   For Loss Of Tooth: Photo document the tooth and mouth [Digital Camera].
2.  **Downlink Photos:** Downlink the photos for the Surgeon.
