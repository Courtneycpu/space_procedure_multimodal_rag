To address the Surgeon's request for pictures of a dental issue, follow these steps:

1. **Inspect and Photograph:**
   - Focus on the affected tooth and any associated issues such as fillings, crowns, or broken fragments.
   - Use a Digital Camera to capture clear images of the tooth and the specific dental problem.

2. **Downlink Photos:**
   - After photographing, downlink the images to the Surgeon using the procedure outlined in 1.480 D4 SETUP – MEDICAL IMAGERY with the Nikon D4 camera.

3. **Tooth-Numbering References:**
   - Refer to "Mouth Anatomy" for anatomic reference images and tooth numbering guidance, accessible through the SSC system.

This process ensures that the Surgeon receives the necessary visual information for assessment.
