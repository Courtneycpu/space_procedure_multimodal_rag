**What to inspect, photograph, and downlink**

1. **Inspect the affected tooth** (and any displaced filling or broken fragment; remove debris if present).  
2. **Photograph the tooth and the dental issue** (e.g., displaced dental filling or broken fragment) with the **Digital Camera**.  
3. **Downlink the photos** to the Surgeon using the medical‑imagery procedure (see *1.480 D4 SETUP – MEDICAL IMAGERY*, ISS PTV: NIKON D4).

**Where to find tooth‑numbering references**

- Consult the **Mouth Anatomy** reference; the tooth‑numbering chart is shown in **Figure 2 – “Tooth Numbering.”**
