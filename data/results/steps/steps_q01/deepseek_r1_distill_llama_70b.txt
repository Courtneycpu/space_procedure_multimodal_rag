Question    : What are the steps to treat a severe allergic reaction?
Timestamp   : 2026-07-06 14:05:40
============================================================

TRACK 1: Pure Text Vector Baseline
------------------------------------------------------------
Sources retrieved : 2
Answer:
1. Remove the Epinephrine (EpiPen) from its protective plastic tubing.  
2. Form a fist around the Epinephrine (EpiPen) with your thumb closest to the safety cap.  
3. Activate the auto-injector by pulling off the safety cap.  
   - WARNING: Do not put fingers over the needle end to prevent injection into the finger.  
   - WARNING: Do not give the shot through clothing to prevent needle contamination.  
4. Using a jabbing motion, press the needle end perpendicular to and firmly against the patient's outer thigh until a click is heard or felt. Hold firmly against the thigh for 10 seconds.  
5. Discard the used Epinephrine (EpiPen) in the original protective plastic tubing and secure it with Gray Tape. Call MCC-H to request a PMC.  
6. If symptoms recur or progress, the Epinephrine (EpiPen) may be repeated every 20 minutes.  
7. Proceed to 2.0.403 ALLERGIC REACTION for further treatment.
