**Steps to treat a severe allergic reaction**

1. **Remove** the Epinephrine (EpiPen) from its protective plastic tubing.  
2. **Form a fist** around the Epinephrine (EpiPen) with the thumb closest to the safety cap.  
3. **Activate** the auto‑injector by pulling off the safety cap.  
4. **Inject**: using a jabbing motion, press the needle end perpendicular to and firmly against the patient’s outer thigh until a click is heard or felt; hold firmly against the thigh for **10 seconds**.  
5. **Discard** the used Epinephrine (EpiPen) in the original protective plastic tubing, secure the tube with **Gray Tape**, and discard. **Call MCC‑H** to request a replacement PMC.  
6. **Repeat** the Epinephrine (EpiPen) dose if symptoms recur or progress, waiting **20 minutes** between doses.  

*(After completing these steps, proceed to reference 2.0.403 ALLERGIC REACTION for further exams, procedures, and treatment.)*

   - Call MCC‑H to request PMC.
