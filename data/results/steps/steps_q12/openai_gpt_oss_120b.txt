**Pain control**

1. Give the crewmate **2 tablets of Ibuprofen (Motrin) – 400 mg each** (Convenience Medication Pack, White).  
2. Give **1 tablet of Hydrocodone/Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP) – 10 mg/300 mg** (Oral Medication Pack, Purple).  

*(If additional analgesia is needed, refer to the “Treating Pain” procedure and consider a nerve block as directed by the Surgeon.)*  

**Eugenol application**

1. **Don nitrile gloves**.  
2. Moisten a small portion of a **4 × 4 in gauze** with Eugenol (Topical & Injectable Medication Pack, Brown).  
3. **Squeeze excess Eugenol** from that gauze onto a second 4 × 4 in gauze or a dry wipe.  
4. Place the **moistened gauze over the exposed pulp**.  
5. Have the patient **hold the gauze with the teeth for 1–2 minutes**.  
6. No additional local anesthesia is required for further Eugenol placement.

**Tissue‑contact warning**

- **Minimize contact of Eugenol with skin or gums** to prevent tissue damage.
