**Pain Control:**
- Take two (2) tablets Ibuprofen (Motrin) (400 mg per tablet) [Convenience Medication Pack (White)]
- Take one (1) tablet Hydrocodone and Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP) (10 mg/300 mg per tablet) [Oral Medication Pack (Purple)]

**Eugenol Steps:**
1. Don Nitrile Gloves
2. Moisten a small portion of a Gauze 4"x4" with Eugenol [Topical & Injectable Medication Pack (Brown)]
3. Squeeze excess Eugenol out of Gauze 4"x4" onto a second piece of Gauze 4"x4" or a Dry Wipe
4. Place moistened portion of Gauze 4"x4" over exposed pulp
5. Have patient hold gauze with teeth for 1 to 2 minutes
6. Avoid chewing on affected side and keep food out of cavity

**Tissue Contact to Avoid:**
- Minimize contact between the Eugenol and skin or gums to prevent tissue damage
