To perform abdominal thrusts on an awake choking patient and determine when to stop, follow these steps:

1. **Position behind the patient.**
2. **Make a fist.**
3. **Place your fist above the patient's belly button but below the ribcage.**
4. **Pull sharply inward and upward.**
5. **Repeat the thrusts until the object is cleared or the patient becomes unconscious.**

Stop performing the abdominal thrusts if the object is cleared or if the patient becomes unconscious. If the patient becomes unconscious, proceed to clear the airway and give breaths as outlined in the procedure.
