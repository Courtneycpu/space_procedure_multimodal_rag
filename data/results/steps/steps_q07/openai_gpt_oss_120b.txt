**Abdominal thrusts (patient is awake)**  

1. Position yourself behind the patient.  
2. Make a fist.  
3. Place the fist **above the belly button but below the ribcage**.  
4. Pull sharply **inward and upward**.  
5. **Repeat** the thrusts **until**:  
   - the obstructing object clears, **or**  
   - the patient becomes unconscious.  

Stop the maneuver as soon as either the airway is cleared or the patient loses consciousness.
