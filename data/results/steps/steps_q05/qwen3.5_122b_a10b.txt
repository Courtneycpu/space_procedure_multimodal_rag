Based on the retrieved emergency procedure for Severe Allergic Reaction (1.101):

**Injection Location:**
- Press the needle end perpendicular to and firmly against the patient's **outer thigh**

**Hold Time:**
- Hold firmly against thigh for **10 seconds** (until a click is heard or felt)

**Repeat Dosing:**
- Epinephrine (EpiPen) may be repeated every **20 minutes** if symptoms recur or progress

**Additional Steps from the Procedure:**
1. Remove Epinephrine (EpiPen) from protective plastic tubing
2. Form a fist around Epinephrine (EpiPen) with thumb closest to safety cap
3. Activate auto injector by pulling off safety cap
4. Using a jabbing motion, press the needle end perpendicular to and firmly against patient's outer thigh until a click is heard or felt
5. Hold firmly against thigh for 10 seconds
6. Discard used Epinephrine (EpiPen) in original protective plastic tubing; secure in tube with Gray Tape and discard
7. Call MCC-H to request PMC

**Warnings:**
- Do not put fingers over needle end of Epinephrine (EpiPen) to prevent injection into finger
- Do not give shot through clothing to prevent needle contamination
