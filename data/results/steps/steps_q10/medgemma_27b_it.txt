Question    : For difficulty breathing, how do I count pulse and respiratory rate, administer oxygen, and give inhaled Albuterol?
Timestamp   : 2026-07-06 14:45:21
============================================================

TRACK 1: Pure Text Vector Baseline
------------------------------------------------------------
Sources retrieved : 1
Answer:
1.  **Count Pulse and Respiratory Rate:**
    *   Measure each by counting for 15 seconds and multiplying by four.
    *   Record data for later reporting to Surgeon.

2.  **Administer Oxygen:**
    *   Retrieve Respiratory Support Pack.
    *   Locate Cue Card in lid pocket.
    *   Provide oxygen via Low Flow Non-Rebreather Mask.
    *   Perform 3.201 RESPIRATORY SUPPORT PACK - CUE CARD, Oxygen via Low Flow Non-Rebreather Mask.

3.  **Administer Inhaled Albuterol (Proventil HFA):**
    *   Shake the Albuterol (Proventil HFA) Inhaler to mix medication and then remove cap [Topical & Injectable Medication Pack (Brown)].
    *   Have patient fully exhale and place inhaler in mouth.
    *   Have patient begin to inhale slowly through the mouth and then activate the inhaler.
    *   Have patient hold breath for 8 to 10 seconds before exhaling.
    *   Deliver a second dose.
