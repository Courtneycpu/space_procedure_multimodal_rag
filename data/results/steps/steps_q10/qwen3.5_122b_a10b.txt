**Emergency Procedure: Difficulty Breathing**

**1. Count Pulse and Respiratory Rate (Step 1.1)**

**2. Administer Oxygen (Step 1.2)**

**3. Administer Inhaled Albuterol (Proventil HFA) (Step 1.3)**
- Shake the Albuterol (Proventil HFA) Inhaler to mix medication and then remove cap [Topical & Injectable Medication Pack (Brown)]
- Have patient fully exhale and place inhaler in mouth
- Have patient begin to inhale slowly through the mouth and then activate the inhaler
- Have patient hold breath for 8 to 10 seconds before exhaling
- Deliver a second dose
