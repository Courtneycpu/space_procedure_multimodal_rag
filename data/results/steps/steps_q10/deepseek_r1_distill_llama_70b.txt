Question    : For difficulty breathing, how do I count pulse and respiratory rate, administer oxygen, and give inhaled Albuterol?
Timestamp   : 2026-07-06 14:56:42
============================================================

TRACK 1: Pure Text Vector Baseline
------------------------------------------------------------
Sources retrieved : 1
Answer:
1. **Count Pulse and Respiratory Rate**  
   - Measure each by counting for 15 seconds and multiplying by four.  
   - Record the data for later reporting to the Surgeon.  

2. **Administer Oxygen**  
   - Retrieve the Respiratory Support Pack.  
   - Locate the Cue Card in the lid pocket.  
   - Provide oxygen via a Low Flow Non-Rebreather Mask.  

3. **Administer Inhaled Albuterol (Proventil HFA)**  
   - Shake the Albuterol (Proventil HFA) Inhaler to mix the medication and remove the cap.  
   - Have the patient fully exhale and place the inhaler in their mouth.  
   - Have the patient begin to inhale slowly through the mouth and activate the inhaler.  
   - Have the patient hold their breath for 8 to 10 seconds before exhaling.  
   - Deliver a second dose.
