To perform CPR with one rescuer available, follow these steps:

1. **ASSESS**  
   - Assess the environment for safety hazards.  
   - Assess the patient's status.  

2. **PERFORM CPR**  
   - Perform 30 chest compressions for every 2 breaths (1 second each breath) for 5 cycles (approximately 2 minutes).  
   - As required, relocate the patient to and restrain them on the Crew Medical Restraint System.  
   - Page an additional crewmember for assistance.  
   - Call MCC-H to request PMC.  

3. **CONTINUE CPR**  
   - Repeat the CPR cycle after each 'Start CPR' prompt from the AED.

   - After the AED prompts "Start CPR," perform a 10-second pulse check.  
   - If no pulse is detected, continue CPR as directed by the AED.

To perform CPR with one rescuer available, follow these steps:

1. **ASSESS**  
   - Assess the environment for safety hazards.  
   - Assess the patient's status.  

2. **DEPLOY AED**  
   - Connect AED electrodes to the patient's chest.  
   - Turn the AED on (press the green button).  
   - Follow the verbal prompts from the AED. If inaudible, read the prompts on the screen.  

3. **MONITOR PATIENT RESPONSE**  
   - After the AED prompts "Start CPR," perform a 10-second pulse check.  
     - If a pulse is detected, stop chest compressions and assist breathing as necessary.  
     - If no pulse is detected, proceed to perform CPR.  

4. **PERFORM CPR**  
   - Perform 30 chest compressions for every 2 breaths (1 second each breath).  
   - Repeat this process after each "Start CPR" prompt from the AED.  

If the patient begins spontaneous breathing or a pulse is detected during CPR, stop chest compressions and assist breathing as necessary.
