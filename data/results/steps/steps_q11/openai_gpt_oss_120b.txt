**Packing the temporary filling material**

1. Puncture the end of the Temporary Tooth Filling container.  
2. Squeeze a small amount of the material onto your finger.  
3. Roll the material on the finger into a ball.  
4. Place the ball into the tooth cavity and gently form it to the tooth.  
5. Wipe off any excess with a 4" × 4" gauze pad.  
6. Have the crewmate firmly bite down on the filling for **2 minutes** to allow it to set to the tooth surfaces.  
7. If needed, remove and smooth any excess using a 4" × 4" gauze pad and a Dental Carver File (G).

**Post‑procedure food and drink restrictions**

- The crewmate may resume drinking **about 1 hour** after placement.  
- Limit solid or hard food on the affected side of the mouth for the **first 12 – 24 hours** while the filling hardens.
