Answer:
In AED Assisted CPR, if the patient is unresponsive and not breathing or has no normal breathing (only gasping), follow these steps:

1. Perform 30 chest compressions for every 2 breaths (1 second each breath) for 5 cycles (approximately 2 minutes).
2. Relocate the patient to and restrain them on the Crew Medical Restraint System.
3. Page an additional crewmember for assistance.
4. Call MCC-H to request PMC.
5. Deploy the AED:
   - Connect AED electrodes to the patient's chest.
   - Turn the AED on (press the green button).
   - Follow the verbal prompts or read the screen if prompts are inaudible.
6. Continue performing CPR as directed by the AED, repeating the cycle of 30 compressions for every 2 breaths until further instructed.
