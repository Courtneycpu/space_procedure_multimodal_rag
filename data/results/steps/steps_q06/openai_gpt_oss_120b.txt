**If the patient is unresponsive and not breathing (or only gasping), proceed to step 3 – Perform CPR.**

1. **Perform CPR** – 30 chest compressions followed by 2 breaths (1 second each breath) for 5 cycles (≈ 2 minutes).  
2. **While performing CPR,** as needed:  
   - Relocate and restrain the patient on the Crew Medical Restraint System.  
   - Page an additional crewmember for assistance.  
   - Call MCC‑H to request a Physician‑Medical‑Crew (PMC) member.
