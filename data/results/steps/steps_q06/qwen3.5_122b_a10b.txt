Based on the retrieved context, if the patient is unresponsive and not breathing or has no normal breathing (only gasping) during AED Assisted CPR, follow these steps:

**3 PERFORM CPR**
1. Perform 30 chest compressions for every 2 breaths (1 second each breath) for 5 cycles (approximately 2 minutes).
2. As required, relocate to and restrain patient on the Crew Medical Restraint System.
3. Page additional crewmember for assistance.
4. Call MCC-H to request PMC.

**4 DEPLOY AED**
1. Connect AED electrodes to patient's chest.
2. Turn AED 'ON' (green) and press.
3. Follow verbal prompts.
4. If verbal prompts inaudible, read prompts on screen.

**5 MONITOR PATIENT RESPONSE**
- After AED prompts 'Start CPR', perform a 10-second pulse check.
- If pulse is detected, proceed to step 6.
- If no pulse is detected, proceed to step 7.
