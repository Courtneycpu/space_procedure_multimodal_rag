**Steps to assist an unconscious choking patient**

1. **Perform abdominal thrusts**  
   1. Position yourself behind the patient.  
   2. Make a fist.  
   3. Place the fist above the belly button but below the ribcage.  
   4. Pull sharply inward and upward.  
   5. Repeat until the object clears **or** the patient becomes unconscious.  

2. **If the patient becomes unconscious, clear the airway and give breaths**  
   1. Open the airway using a **head‑tilt, chin‑lift**.  
   2. Inspect the mouth for a foreign object and remove it **if it is easily accessible**.  
   3. Pinch the patient’s nose and give breaths.  

3. **Proceed to AED‑assisted CPR** (follow the AED‑assisted CPR procedure after giving breaths).
