Answer:
**Pulse Check:**
- After the AED prompts "Start CPR," perform a **10-second pulse check**

**When to Stop Compressions:**
- Stop chest compressions if a **pulse is detected**
- Stop chest compressions if the **patient begins spontaneous breathing**
- If pulse returns, also stop medication administration and give breaths

**If No Pulse is Detected:**
- Continue performing CPR (30 chest compressions for every 2 breaths, 1 second each breath)
- Repeat the pulse check after each "Start CPR" prompt from the AED
