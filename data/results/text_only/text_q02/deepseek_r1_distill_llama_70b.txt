Answer:
After completing AED assisted CPR, follow these steps:

1. **Monitor Patient Response:**
   - After the AED prompts 'Start CPR', perform a 10-second pulse check.

2. **Assess Pulse:**
   - If a pulse is detected:
     - Stop chest compressions.
     - Assist breathing as necessary.
     - If a second rescuer is assisting, stop medication administration.
     - Proceed to **1.103 ALS ALGORITHM, block 13, page 5, Respiratory Assessment**.
   - If no pulse is detected:
     - Continue performing CPR as instructed by the AED.

This procedure ensures appropriate care based on the patient's response after AED use.
