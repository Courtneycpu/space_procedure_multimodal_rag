Based on the retrieved context, after AED assisted CPR, the procedure depends on patient response:

**If patient begins spontaneous breathing or a pulse is detected:**
1. Stop chest compressions
2. Assist breathing as necessary
3. If second rescuer assisting, stop medication administration
4. Go to 1.103 ALS ALGORITHM, block 13, page 5, Respiratory Assessment (SODF: MED CL: EMERGENCY)

**If no pulse is detected:**
1. Perform 30 chest compressions for every 2 breaths (1 second each breath) until prompted by AED
2. Repeat monitoring (block 5) after each 'Start CPR' prompt from AED

**Monitoring Process:**
- After AED prompts 'Start CPR', perform a 10-second pulse check
- If pulse detected → proceed to ASSIST PATIENT (Step 6)
- If no pulse → continue PERFORM CPR (Step 7)

**Step 7 - PERFORM CPR (if no pulse detected):**
1. Perform 30 chest compressions for every 2 breaths (1 second each breath) until prompted by AED.
2. Repeat block 5 after each 'Start CPR' prompt from AED.

**Additional Note:** If two rescuers are available and AED is deployed, go to 1.103 ALS ALGORITHM, block 1 (SODF: MED CL: EMERGENCY).
