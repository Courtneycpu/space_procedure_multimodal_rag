**After completing AED‑assisted CPR, follow the “Monitor Patient Response” block:**

1. **Perform a 10‑second pulse check** after the AED prompts “Start CPR.”  
2. **If a pulse is detected** → go to **Step 6 – Assist Patient** (stop chest compressions, assist breathing as needed, and discontinue any medication administration if a second rescuer is present).  
3. **If no pulse is detected** → go to **Step 7 – Perform CPR** (30 compressions / 2 breaths) and repeat the monitoring block after each subsequent “Start CPR” prompt from the AED.
