Answer:
If the medicine does not flow through the Intraosseous needle, follow these steps:

1. Go to block 20, page 7, Intraosseous Needle Troubleshooting.
2. If two attempts at placing the Intraosseous device have not been made:
   - Prepare for a second Intraosseous device insertion using the other leg.
   - Retrieve the Intraosseous Device Start Kit with the second Intraosseous Device [Emergency Medical Treatment Pack (Red)].
   - Proceed to block 1, page 1, Intraosseous Device Insertion, and perform the steps using the other leg.
3. If two attempts have been made:
   - Attach a Syringe 10 mL to the IV Cap on the failed Intraosseous Device(s).
   - Pull back on the plunger of the Syringe 10 mL by at least 5 mL, then disconnect the Syringe 10 mL from the blue IV Cap. Blood or fluid may or may not be seen. This action will potentially clear bone fragments from the needle.
   - Attempt to re-administer the medication.
     - If successful, go to block 9, page 3.
     - If unsuccessful, support AED Assisted CPR and report the results to the Surgeon if able.
