**Loss of Tooth – Bleeding, Pain, and Documentation**

1. **Control bleeding**  
   - Apply direct pressure to the tooth socket with a **4" × 4" gauze pad**.  
   - Maintain continuous pressure until bleeding stops.

2. **Treat pain**  
   - **Mild‑to‑moderate pain:**  
     - Take **2 tablets Ibuprofen (Motrin) – 400 mg each** (Convenience Medication Pack, White).  
     - Take **1 tablet Hydrocodone/Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP) – 10 mg/300 mg** (Oral Medication Pack, Purple).  
   - **Severe or immediate relief needed:**  
     - Perform the **Nerve Block procedure (2.6.301 DENTAL PROCEDURE – NERVE BLOCK)** before or in addition to medication.

3. **Document the injury**  
   - Place the extracted tooth in a **Ziplock bag** and temporarily stow it.  
   - **Photo‑document** the tooth and surrounding mouth using a **digital camera**.  

*All items listed (gauze, medication packs, Ziplock bag, digital camera) are provided in the standard dental kits.*

**Loss of Tooth – Procedure**

1. **Control bleeding**  
   - Apply direct pressure to the tooth socket with a **Gauze 4"x4"**.  
   - Hold continuous pressure until bleeding stops.

2. **Treat pain**  
   - Take **two (2) tablets Ibuprofen (Motrin) – 400 mg each** from the **Convenience Medication Pack (White)**.  
   - Take **one (1) tablet Hydrocodone and Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP) – 10 mg/300 mg** from the **Oral Medication Pack (Purple)**.  
   - *If pain is severe or immediate relief is required*: perform a **dental nerve block (procedure 2.6.301)** before medication.

3. **Document the injury**  
   - **Place the extracted tooth in a Ziplock bag** and temporarily stow it.  
   - **Photo‑document the tooth and mouth** using a **Digital Camera**.  
   - **Downlink the photos to the Surgeon** via the **SSC** (refer to 1.480 D4 SETUP – MEDICAL IMAGERY).  
   - After downlink, **discard the Ziplock bag**.
