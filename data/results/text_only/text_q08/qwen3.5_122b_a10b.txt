**1. CONTROLLING BLEEDING**
1. Apply direct pressure to the tooth socket with Gauze 4"x4"
2. Hold continuous pressure until bleeding stops

**2. TREATING PAIN**
1. Take two (2) tablets Ibuprofen (Motrin) (400 mg per tablet) from Convenience Medication Pack (White)
2. Take one (1) tablet Hydrocodone and Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP) (10 mg/300 mg per tablet) from Oral Medication Pack (Purple)
3. If pain severe or immediate relief required, perform 2.6.301 DENTAL PROCEDURE - NERVE BLOCK

**3. DOCUMENTING THE INJURY**
1. Place tooth in Ziplock Bag and temporarily stow
2. Photo document tooth and mouth using Digital Camera
3. Downlink photos for Surgeon

**DENTAL PROCEDURE - LOSS OF TOOTH (2.6.305)**

**1. CONTROLLING BLEEDING**
- Apply direct pressure to the tooth socket with Gauze 4"x4"
- Hold continuous pressure until bleeding stops

**2. TREATING PAIN**
- Take two (2) tablets Ibuprofen (Motrin) (400 mg per tablet) from Convenience Medication Pack (White)
- Take one (1) tablet Hydrocodone and Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP) (10 mg/300 mg per tablet) from Oral Medication Pack (Purple)
- If pain severe or immediate relief required, perform 2.6.301 DENTAL PROCEDURE - NERVE BLOCK
- If required, refer to 2.0.402 TREATING PAIN

**3. TOOTH STORAGE**
- Place tooth in Ziplock Bag and temporarily stow

**4. PHOTO DOCUMENTING BROKEN TOOTH**
- Photo document tooth and mouth using Digital Camera
- Downlink photos for Surgeon
- To view anatomic reference images of the mouth and teeth, refer to Mouth Anatomy

**ITEMS NEEDED:**
- Convenience Medication Pack (White): Ibuprofen (Motrin)
- Medical Supply Pack (Green): Gauze 4"x4"
- Oral Medication Pack (Purple): Hydrocodone and Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP)
- Digital Camera
- SSC
- Ziplock Bag
