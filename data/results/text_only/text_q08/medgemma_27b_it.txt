Question    : For loss of tooth, how do I control bleeding, treat pain, and document the injury?
Timestamp   : 2026-07-06 14:40:28
============================================================

TRACK 1: Pure Text Vector Baseline
------------------------------------------------------------
Sources retrieved : 3
Answer:
1.  **Controlling Bleeding:**
    *   Apply direct pressure to the tooth socket with Gauze 4"x4".
    *   Hold continuous pressure until bleeding stops.

2.  **Treating Pain:**
    *   Take two (2) tablets Ibuprofen (Motrin) (400 mg per tablet) [Convenience Medication Pack (White)].
    *   Take one (1) tablet Hydrocodone and Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP) (10 mg/300 mg per tablet) [Oral Medication Pack (Purple)].
    *   If pain is severe or immediate relief is required, perform 2.6.301 DENTAL PROCEDURE - NERVE BLOCK, all.

3.  **Documenting Injury:**
    *   Photo document tooth and mouth [Digital Camera].
    *   Place tooth in Ziplock Bag and temporarily stow.
