Answer:
For loss of tooth, follow these steps:

1. **Controlling Bleeding**  
   - Apply direct pressure to the tooth socket with Gauze 4"x4".  
   - Hold continuous pressure until bleeding stops.  

2. **Treating Pain**  
   - Take two (2) tablets of Ibuprofen (Motrin) (400 mg per tablet) from the Convenience Medication Pack (White).  
   - Take one (1) tablet of Hydrocodone and Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP) (10 mg/300 mg per tablet) from the Oral Medication Pack (Purple).  
   - If pain is severe or immediate relief is required, perform procedure 2.6.301 DENTAL PROCEDURE - NERVE BLOCK.  

3. **Documenting the Injury**  
   - Photo document the tooth and mouth using a Digital Camera.
