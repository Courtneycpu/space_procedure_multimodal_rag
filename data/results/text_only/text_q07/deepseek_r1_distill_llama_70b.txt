The conditions under which CPR termination should be considered in the ALS Algorithm are:

1. At least 10 minutes of CPR since the last dose of medication.
2. No pulse or spontaneous breathing.
3. At least 45 minutes elapsed since the start of resuscitation.

These conditions must all apply for CPR termination to be considered. Additionally, coordination with a surgeon, if available, is recommended.
