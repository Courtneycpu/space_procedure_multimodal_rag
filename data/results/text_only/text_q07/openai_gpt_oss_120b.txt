**CPR termination is considered when all of the following conditions are met (after coordinating with the Surgeon, if available):**

- At least **10 minutes of CPR** have elapsed since the last dose of medication, **and**
- The patient has **no pulse and no spontaneous breathing**, **and**
- At least **45 minutes have elapsed** since the start of resuscitation.
