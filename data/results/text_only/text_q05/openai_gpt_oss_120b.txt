Answer:
- **Continue CPR** – do not stop chest compressions while the patient is unresponsive, not breathing normally, and showing no signs of circulation.  
- **Replace the AED battery** as soon as practicable (at the earliest opportunity).  
- **Notify MCC‑H** of the battery condition.  

*Note:* The AED can still deliver up to 30 shocks and provide monitoring for about 75 minutes when the battery indicator shows one bar.
