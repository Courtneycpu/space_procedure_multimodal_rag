Question    : During CPR the AED battery warning came on. Should we stop resuscitation to change it, or keep working while handling the battery problem?
Timestamp   : 2026-07-06 13:11:08
============================================================

TRACK 1: Pure Text Vector Baseline
------------------------------------------------------------
Sources retrieved : 2
Answer:
1. Continue CPR if the patient is not responding, not breathing normally, and showing no signs of circulation.
2. Replace the AED battery at the earliest opportunity.
3. Notify MCC-H.

Do not stop CPR to change the battery; continue resuscitation efforts while handling the battery replacement as soon as possible.
