Based on the retrieved AED Troubleshooting procedure, when a battery warning comes on during CPR:

1. **Continue CPR** if the patient is not responding, not breathing normally, and showing no signs of circulation
2. Check Battery indicator in MAIN DISPLAY
3. Replace AED Battery at earliest opportunity
4. Notify MCC-H

You should **keep working while handling the battery problem** - do not stop resuscitation to change the battery. Replace the battery at the earliest opportunity while continuing CPR.
