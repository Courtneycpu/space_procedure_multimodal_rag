2.6.401 DENTAL TREATMENT - TOOTH PAIN
(MED CL/SpX-6 - ALL/FIN 2/T)
Page 1 of 1 page

OBJECTIVE:
To treat dental pain.

ITEMS:
Convenience Medication Pack (White):
Ibuprofen (Motrin)
Oral Medication Pack (Purple):
Hydrocodone and Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP)

1. TREATING PAIN
Take Medication
Take two (2) tablets Ibuprofen (Motrin) (400 mg per tablet) [Convenience Medication
Pack (White)] and one (1) tablet Hydrocodone and Acetaminophen (Vicodin HP) (10
mg/300 mg per tablet) [Oral Medication Pack (Purple)] for pain control.
If required, refer to 2.0.402 TREATING PAIN, all (SODF: MED CL: EXAMS,
PROCEDURES, AND TREATMENT).

If pain severe or immediate relief required
Go to 2.6.301 DENTAL PROCEDURE - NERVE BLOCK, all (SODF: MED CL:
EXAMS, PROCEDURES, AND TREATMENT).
