If one rescuer available -> 1 ASSESS

If two rescuers available
Rescuer 1 -> 1 ASSESS
Rescuer 2 -> Deploy AED, then: Go to 1.103 ALS ALGORITHIM, block 1 (SODF: MED CL: EMERGENCY)

1 ASSESS
1. Assess environment for safety hazards.
2. Assess patient status.

-> Patient unresponsive and breathing normally -> 2 
-> Patient unresponsive and not breathing or no normal breathing (only gasping) -> 3


2 TRANSPORT / PROVIDE CARE
1. Relocate to and restrain patient on the Crew Medical Restraint System.
2. Page additional crewmember for assistance.
3. Call MCC-H to request PMC.
4. Go to 1.103 ALS ALGORITHM, block 13, page 5, Respiratory Assessment (SODF: MED CL: EMERGENCY).

3 PERFORM CPR
Perform 30 chest compressions for every 2 breaths (1 second each breath) for 5 cycles (approximately 2 minutes).
1. As required, relocate to and restrain patient on the Crew Medical Restraint System.
2. Page additional crewmember for assistance.
3. Call MCC-H to request PMC. -> 4

4 DEPLOY AED
1. Connect AED electrodes to patient's chest. (Figure 1)
2. AED 'ON' (green) -> Press
3. Follow verbal prompts.
4. If verbal prompts inaudible, read prompts on screen. -> 5

5 MONITOR PATIENT RESPONSE
After AED prompts 'Start CPR'
Perform a 10-second pulse check.
Is pulse detected?
-> Yes -> 6 
-> No -> 7

6 ASSIST PATIENT
If patient begins spontaneous breathing or a pulse is detected
Stop chest compressions.
Assist breathing as necessary.
If second rescuer assisting
Stop medication administration.
Go to 1.103 ALS ALGORITHM, block 13, page 5, Respiratory Assessment (SODF: MED CL: EMERGENCY).

7 PERFORM CPR
Perform 30 chest compressions for every 2 breaths (1 second each breath) until prompted by AED.
Repeat block 5 after each 'Start CPR' prompt from AED.
